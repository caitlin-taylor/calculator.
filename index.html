Yummy Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 Apple
  • small slice of Ginger
  • tbsp Lemon juice
  • tsp Linseed
  • tsp Chia Seeds

Method:

  1. Peel Banana and carrot, add to blender
  2. Half apple and slice 1cm thick slide of ginger, add to blender
  3. Squeeze half a lemon into blender
  4. Add seeds and blend!
